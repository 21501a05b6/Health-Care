INTRODUCTION TO PREGNANCY

Pregnancy, a transformative period in a woman’s life, signifies the conception and development of one or more offspring in her womb. It is a remarkable journey that typically spans around nine months and can be categorized into three trimesters, each characterized by unique physiological changes and developmental milestones. The magic starts with fertilization, when a sperm from the male unites with an egg from the female to form a zygote. This single cell then undergoes rapid divisions and transforms into a multi-cellular embryo, eventually implanting itself into the uterus.
